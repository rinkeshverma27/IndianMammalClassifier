Is it dangerous to humans?
WildGuide
Bengal Tigers are wild predators and can be dangerous, especially "man-eaters" in the Sundarbans. However, most tigers avoid humans. You're far safer on a forest safari than on a busy road — they're shy, not aggressive by nature.

Where's the best place to see one?
WildGuide
Ranthambore in Rajasthan has the highest sighting success rates — tigers there are habituated to safari vehicles. Kanha and Bandhavgarh in Madhya Pradesh are also excellent. Best season: March–June.
